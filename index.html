Rumblers is a car club

It's not for new cars or garage queens. Rumblers is for cars that are driven, parked outside, and used. The club is for cars that are simple enough to fix by hand but complex enough to give you a smile on the road.

  • Next

It all started in 1931

In the early days of modifying cars, a group of 11 founding members started a club in California. The goal was simple; have fun and drive fast. Over 70 years later, Rumblers members are still around. Today any car is welcome to apply for membership.

Become a Rumbler

Any running, pre 1975 car is qualified to apply to join the Rumblers. Only 100 cars are accepted each year. Once a year members are invited to meet in California for an annual drive.

Go Fast and Far
